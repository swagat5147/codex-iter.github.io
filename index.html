About Us

Codex is the official coding community of Institute of Technical Education and Research.

We bring in budding coders as well as the experienced ones and give them a platform to meet, share their knowledge and work on building awesome projects.

WE BELIEVE IN GREAT IDEAS

We believe that everyone can come up with great ideas and work on them to create awesome projects.

Every company looks for projects in your CV and we are going to do a lot of them.

You'll get guidance from expert faculties and alumni and you get to work with trending languages and topics such as Python, Javascript and IOT, Machine Learning etc

You'll get to present your projects to an audience and enhance your problem solving skills
